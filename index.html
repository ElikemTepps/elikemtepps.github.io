Eth Accra Hackathon

ETHAccra - premier Ethereum hackathon in the heart of West Africa. Created by builders, for builders.Through events, workshops, and a strong network, ETHAccra is building a vibrant ecosystem that supports new ideas and collaboration. Join us as we work towards a more inclusive and decentralized future. ETHAccra is an active and inclusive community in Ghana, West Africa, bringing together developers, researchers, designers, and newcomers interested in web3 and blockchain technologies.
